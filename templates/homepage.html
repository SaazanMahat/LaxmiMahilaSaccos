WELCOME TO LAXMI MAHILA SACCOS

“सहकारीको बिकास सँगै सामाजिक बिकास मा समर्पित”

Since its establishment in Chaitra 13, 2055 BS, Laxmi Saccos has been giving its best to make itself a "Community Based Sustainable, Excellent and Professional Women's Cooperative" by constantly helping all the women of Panauti, Dhulikhel and Banepa Municipality to raise their living standards and increase self-reliance. To provide various economic, social and important information of this organization to the members in a timely and transparent manner, we have developed this website. All our members have their important advice, suggestions and we heartily request all the members and well wishers to show solidarity for the growth and development of this organization with the expectation of continuous support for its creative works. . .

First of all, I would like to warmly welcome you to the official site of Laxmi Mahila Saving and Credit Cooperative Society Limited. Established in 2056 B.S, this organization carries the main motive of making women in the society self-reliant.
